Supply Chain Management

Supply Chain Case Study – Premium Food Co.

During a simulated COVID-19 ingredient shortage, I used Multi-Criteria Decision Analysis in Excel to evaluate four supplier strategies across gross margin, on-time revenue, and environmental impact. I recommended Prioritize Key Customers as the optimal approach to protect long-term relationships while maintaining profitability. Findings were delivered as a team presentation.

Academic Research

OLS Causal Inference: Does Experience Increase Salary?

I sourced and cleaned an employee salary dataset from Kaggle, then built regression models in R to test whether work experience causally increases salary. My baseline model confirmed a statistically significant relationship. Adding age as a control revealed that seniority partially explains the experience-salary link. Results were translated into plain-language business insights for a class audience.

Vocalist — Acoustic Series Live Band, Seattle

Singing has been part of me since first grade. I can not really explain it, but when I am on stage, everything else just fades out. It is the one place where I feel completely like myself.

I sing as a lead vocalist for an acoustic live band here in Seattle, a team of 8 people: 3 vocalists and 5 instrumentalists on keyboard, piano, guitar, bass, and drums. We perform at private events, and honestly the show days are some of the most memorable moments of my life. The backstage chaos, the adrenaline right before we go on, the way everything just clicks when the music comes together... those are the moments I hold onto.

What I did not expect was how much being in a band would shape the way I work. Coordinating with 7 people, staying calm when things go sideways, and delivering when the pressure is high taught me things no classroom could.

Flowers & Floral Arrangements

I honestly can not pinpoint exactly when flowers became a thing for me. They just always have been. For the longest time I thought buying flowers was kind of wasteful — they are pretty for a few days and then they are gone, right? But somewhere along the way, getting a little older I think, my perspective completely shifted.

Now I see them differently. There is something genuinely calming about having fresh flowers around. Looking at them just makes me feel better, and I think that is enough of a reason. I do not need them to last forever to appreciate them while they are here.

Trader Joe's is my go-to spot. Good quality, great prices, and they always have something new to try. It has become a little ritual for me and honestly one of my favorite kinds of self-care.
